# 8. Success Metrics and KPIs

## V1 Launch Success Metrics

- **Activation:** 100+ free signups within first 14 days post-launch
- **Trial→paid conversion:** 5-10% (industry norm for low-cost SaaS)
- **Time-to-first-search:** <60 seconds from signup (drop-off metric)
- **Time-to-first-export:** <5 minutes (the "ah-ha" moment)
- **Data-quality satisfaction:** >70% of paid users return for month 2

## North-Star Metric

**Weekly Active Searchers (WAS):** users who perform ≥1 search + 1 export in a given week. This captures actual value delivery better than MRR alone.

## Tracking Implementation

- Plausible or PostHog (privacy-friendly) for product analytics
- Chargily webhook events logged for revenue tracking
- Weekly export: free signups, paid conversions, search count, export count, MRR, churn

## Monthly Targets (illustrative — NOT a ceiling)

- Month 1 (post-FB-test): 30-50 paid users, ~50K-75K DZD MRR
- Month 3: 100-200 paid users, ~150-300K DZD MRR
- Month 6: 300-500 paid users, V1.5 mid-tier pushing ARPU up, ~500K-1M DZD MRR
- Month 12: V2 launched, sequences + CRM + WhatsApp integration → ARPU stretch to 5,000-15,000 DZD; MRR potentially 2-5M DZD

**Note:** The user explicitly removed the 45K DZD/month ceiling. The numbers above illustrate the path, not a cap. The V2/V3 upsell ladder is the unbounded-MRR mechanism.
