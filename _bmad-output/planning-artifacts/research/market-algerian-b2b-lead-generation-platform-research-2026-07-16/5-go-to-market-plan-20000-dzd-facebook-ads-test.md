# 5. Go-to-Market Plan — 20,000 DZD Facebook Ads Test

## Budget Allocation (20,000 DZD ≈ $150)

The 20K DZD test budget is small (~$150 USD). Effectiveness depends entirely on narrow targeting + clear wedge message. Don't spread thin.

| Allocation | Budget | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Audience 1 — SMB founders in Algiers/Oran/Constantine (mega-cities) | 8,000 DZD (40%) | Primary TAM; wedge message in Arabic + French |
| Audience 2 — Marketing freelancers & small agencies | 6,000 DZD (30%) | Higher-volume but more reluctant; experiment price-led |
| Audience 3 — Lookalike of early signups (built after week 1) | 4,000 DZD (20%) | Most efficient once you have 50+ signups |
| Retargeting — site visitors who didn't sign up | 2,000 DZD (10%) | Cheap, high-intent |

**Angle 2 — "Your prospecting stack in one Algerian tool" (agencies/freelancers)**
- Visual: Side-by-side price table yours vs DzLeads vs Linkiw
- Headline FR: *"Vos prospects B2B en Algérie — 1500 DA/mois — export CSV en 1 clic"*

**Angle 3 — Retargeting (visitors)**

## Funnel Design

```
FB ad → trilingual landing page (auto-detect language by browser/UA)
→ free signup (Google OAuth + email, no card)
→ 15 free credits activated instantly
→ first-search success pop-up: "Try with a company you know — like Cevital, Djezzy, or Sonatrach!"
→ at credit 13/15 used → email + WhatsApp "Want 200 more for 1500 DZD?"
→ Chargily checkout (CIB/EDahabia)
→ activation: send first CSV export to in-app email
→ retention email at week 2: new saved-search counts
```

## Per-Ad KPIs (what success looks like for the 20K DZD test)

- **CPC target:** < 25 DZD ($0.18) — Algerian FB CPC norm is $0.10–$0.30
- **CTR target:** > 1.5% (Algerian B2B FB ad norm ~1.0-1.5%; V1's wedge message should drive higher)
- **Signup cost target:** < 200 DZD per signup ($1.50)
- **Paid conversion target:** 5-10% of free signups → paid within 14 days
- **Break-even path:** at 1,500 DZD ARPU, a paid user must cost < 1,500 DZD to acquire (CAC < ARPU)
- **20K DZD test success threshold:** 100+ free signups + 10+ paid users from 20K spend = train-off for monthly scaling

## Channels Beyond FB Ads (organic flywheels)

- **FB business groups** — Founder Q&A posting in "Algerian Entrepreneurs Network" / "Freelancers DZ" / specific industry groups
- **LinkedIn posts** by Akram (founder-led content); "build in public" weekly posts
- **YouTube tutorials** — record 3 short videos "How to find clients in Algeria" (in Arabic + French) — discovered in competitor analysis that incumbents are weak here
- **WhatsApp broadcast lists** — once you have 50+ happy users, offer them a "refer a friend → 50 free credits" loop
