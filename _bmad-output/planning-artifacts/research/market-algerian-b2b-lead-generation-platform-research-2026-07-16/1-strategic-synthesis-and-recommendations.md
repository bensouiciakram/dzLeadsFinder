# 1. Strategic Synthesis and Recommendations

## Recommended V1 Positioning Statement

> **"Trilingual Algerian lead database — every official wilaya, verified contacts, easy export — from 1,500 DZD/mo."**

Short version for FB ad copy:
> **"قاعدة بيانات الشركات الجزائرية — 58 ولاية، 3 لغات، تصدير CSV — من 1500 دج/شهر"**

## V1 Must-Haves (from pain-point analysis)

1. ✅ Search + filter (industry, wilaya, seniority, company size, keywords)
2. ✅ People + Company search (matching DzLeads' two-database model)
3. ✅ Trilingual UI (AR/FR/EN) with locale-conscious content (not just string translation)
4. ✅ Accurate 58-wilaya taxonomy (published openly, defensible against the 48/69 discrepancies)
5. ✅ 15 free credits on signup (matches/beats both incumbents; no credit card required)
6. ✅ CSV/Excel export (universal buyer expectation, verified)
7. ✅ Chargily CIB/EDahabia payment (matches DzLeads; required table-stakes)
8. ✅ WhatsApp support even at the lowest paid tier (underserved by Linkiw)
9. ✅ Public "/how-we-verify" page
10. ✅ Founder narrative ("Made by Akram in Algiers") on /about — both incumbents obscure founders

## V1 Explicit Non-Goals (de-risk 3-4 week MVP)

- ❌ CRM / lists persistence beyond saved searches (Linkiw is winning here; don't compete in V1)
- ❌ Email sequences / outreach automation
- ❌ WhatsApp outreach integration (V2)
- ❌ AI agent / auto-categorization (Linkiw is building this; don't compete yet)
- ❌ API (technical buyers are <10% of V1 TAM)
- ❌ Team seats / shared workspaces
- ❌ International / cross-border data
- ❌ Mobile app

## Recommended Revenue Ladder (V1 → V3)

**V1 (MVP, this build):** Single paid tier + free trial. 1,500 DZD/mo for 200 credits. Goal: establish the wedge.

**V1.5 (4-6 weeks post-V1 launch):** Add a mid-tier (~5,000 DZD/mo, 750 credits) for heavier solo users + light agencies. Add credit rollover + saved searches.

**V2 (3-6 months):** Add a 15,000 DZD/mo tier (~3,000 credits) with sequences, basic CRM, WhatsApp-integration beta. This attacks Linkiw's PME/Agences tier head-on. Add annual pricing (–30%) matching DzLeads' "Save 38%" framing.

**V3 (6-12 months):** Custom + API + team seats. Cross-border data for exporters. This is the unbounded-MRR growth phase.
