## Methodology Disclosure

This research was conducted under the bmad-market-research workflow (6 steps: init → behavior → pain points → decisions → competitive → synthesis). All competitor pricing/feature/UI facts are HIGH confidence (live-site fetches on 2026-07-16). Algerian buyer-psychology and decision-timeline inferences are MEDIUM confidence (analyst extrapolation grounded in competitor UX patterns and Algerian B2B norms; no cited formal buyer survey). Long-term market evolution inferences (18+ months) are LOW confidence (speculative).

## Limitations

- No direct buyer-survey data (would require Algerian SME sampling, beyond this research scope)
- G2/Capterra reviews for Apollo blocked by scraping protections — Apollo's customer-satisfaction analysis relied on Apollo's own FAQ + public marketing content
- Legal assessment of CNRC/Pages Jaunes data sourcing is *risk-flagging only* and must be confirmed by an Algerian lawyer before commercialization
- Wilaya reform data referenced is post-2021 (48 → 58 official wilayas); further reforms possible
